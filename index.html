A Study on HDB Resale Prices for new Downtown Line Stations

Alex Ng

2019-05-04

Chapter 1 Abstract

A study to find out how HDB resale prices in Singapore is affected by the new MRT stations within 1km radius using historical data. A hedonic regression is performed using linear and log-linear models and has established a mixed relationship for units located within 1km radius. K means clustering is done to find other relationships.
