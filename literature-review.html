A Study on HDB Resale Prices for new Downtown Line Stations

Chapter 3 Literature Review

Studies done to find the effect on price with a new MRT on the Downtown Line from NUS Economics thesis. He looked at all stations on Downtown Line 2 and examine the resale price effect using regression. The key result is an average initial increase in nearby resale prices by 1.9 per cent, construction period by 3 per cent, and 7 per cent for those within 1km. A difference-in-difference approach is used to analyse the data (Chia and Lim 2016). A hedonic price model is done on HDB resale flat data to compare across electoral lines includes floor square metres squared, distance to expressway, MUP upgrading completion and proximity to bus interchange (Sue and Wong 2010).
